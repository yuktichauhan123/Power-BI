Mobile Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

This Power BI project provides a detailed analysis of Mobile sales performance.  
It showcases key business insights such as total sales, customer behavior, payment methods, and ratings ‚Äî all in an interactive and visually appealing dashboard.


Project Overview

The Mobile Sales Dashboard visualizes important metrics for mobile sales, helping stakeholders track performance and identify key trends.
Key Insights Displayed:
-Total Sales: 769M  
-Total Quantity Sold: 19K  
-Transactions: 4K  
-Average Price: 40K  


Dashboard Features

1. Sales Overview
- Tracks total sales, quantities, and transactions.
- Displays the average selling price for all models.

2. Trend Analysis
- Monthly trend of total quantity sold throughout the year.

3. Payment Method Distribution
- Pie chart showing the share of transactions by payment types (UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, Cash).

4. Geographic Insights
- Interactive map showing sales distribution across major Indian cities.

5. Top Performing Mobile Models
- Bar chart of top 5 models by total sales:
  - iPhone SE (60M)
  - OnePlus Nord (58M)
  - Galaxy Note 20 (56M)
  - Vivo Y51 (55M)
  - Galaxy S21 (53M)

6. Customer Ratings
- Bar chart and trend chart visualizing customer satisfaction levels.
